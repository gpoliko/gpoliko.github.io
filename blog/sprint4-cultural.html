Research Growth Mindset

What is it and why is it relevant?

A growth mindset is the idea that our capacities can be developed further. Adopting a humble posture of learning and a willingness to accept new challenges, can greatly assist in individual growth. This is relevant, especially in a career where we are constantly challenged, succeeding, and failing. It allows us to see our mistakes or shortfalls as learning opportunities, instead of failures. An important aspect of having a growth mindset is active reflection on oneself. Acknowledging the success, as well as identifying how we can improve.


Did anything surprise you?

I did have a previous understanding of neuroplasticity and a growth mindset. However, the change for me was the need to apply a growth mindset with my studies. It can be easy to get complacent with previous knowledge and education. This is a trap I do not want to fall into.


How will I integrate a growth mindset into my learning journey?

The most difficult aspect for me is adopting a humble posture of learning. This is something I very conscious of. Regardless of my previous understandings or education, I will endeavor to see every activity or exercise as something foreign and new, and to enjoy the process of learning.
